Negotiating with Publishers

While negotiating the terms of your contract with publishers, terms of the Library License will be agreed upon.

Performance-Based Licensing

When sales of the work drop beneath the agreed threshold, the Library License takes effect.

Co-existence

With the license in effect, full digital rights are given to recognized libraries. Publishers maintain exclusive commercial rights.

Access

Libraries continue their mission to provide information resources to the public.
