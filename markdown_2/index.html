When we came together Wednesday, March 11th, 9:15 for final salon before Spring Break, everything was different.

Everyone at the Table

Welcome to the Greenhouse🌱. We’re a mostly no-credit, non-departmental creative salon inside Olin College. We grow young ideas, not plants. We Greenhouse-folk are a crosscutting bunch - staff, faculty and students alike. At best we come together as a production company. We produce culture that builds our community.
